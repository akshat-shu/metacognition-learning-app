### When I first saw the problem statement, I thought the real task was...
- to build some kind of app for students which would help them in their education, like complete their homework, or allow them to learn new topics in an interactive way, while not hiding the reasoning process. I thought the app would be like a tutor, which would help students understand concepts and solve problems, rather than just giving them answers.

### The first thing I personally wanted to do was...
- I wanted to come up with a solid idea of what our team was going to build, after understanding the problem and researching other solutions. I came up with the idea of a gamified platform which would have different levels, challenges and currencies along with different games which would teach students different aspects of learning concepts in an AI-Rich world. For example, some game might give two responses to a question and ask the student to find the response which is correct and identify where the other is hallucinating.

### The first thing our team actually did was...
- Our team started by brainstorming ideas and looking at research papers to understand the problem better. We made a [Google Doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xOgWy5HOS4_QZghjYmMs60dio9oUcz2WBl8elBs5h04) to jot down our ideas and thoughts. 

### That helped because...
- We got to take a look and understand about all of the different perspectives and ideas that each of us had. We also got to understand the problem better and see what other people were doing in this space. It also helped us to come up with a more concrete idea of what we wanted to build.

### That hurt because...
- We spent a lot of time brainstorming (more than one day) and we didn't start building anything until the third day. We also got a bit overwhelmed with all the different ideas and perspectives, which made it hard to converge on a single idea.

### One assumption I made about teenagers, learning or AI was...
That AI would be able to quickly complete the implementation of our idea, which would allow us to spend more time focusing on the design. AI delivered on that assumption, it was able to implement all of the features correctly and quickly.
